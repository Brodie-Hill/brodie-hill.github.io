Defender: World is a remake of the arcade game Defender in GameMaker Studio.
The player must both protect the civilians from abduction, while also avoiding the death of their pilots.
If you run out of reserve pilots, you loose, and if the alien intruders eliminate all of your civilians, you also loose.
There are three 'technologies' you can use to help you:
• Ejection: If you are about to crash, and have reserve pilots left, you can eject and recover your ejectee in the next life.
• Hyper-Drive Teleport: You can teleport to a random high-atmosphere location to try your luck in evading a bad situation. But this could put you in a neven worse one.
• S-Bomb: This feature comes straight from the original game. Activating an S-Bomb eliminates all on-screen enemies.

Defender: World features realistic aerodynamics, allowing for dynamic and fun movement play like gliding or aerobraking, as well as many unique enemies each with their own behaviour.
